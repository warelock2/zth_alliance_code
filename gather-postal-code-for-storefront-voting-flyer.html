We Need Your Help

 
If you want to be able to directly spend honest money with zero transaction history like physical Gold, physical Silver, and Pirate Chain privacy cryptocurrency, it is up to you to reach out to your local merchants and other interested parties to get them on board with the ZTH Alliance. If you're able to do that with a simple conversation, then great. However, if a merchant says they need to see real data reflecting local customer interest in directly spending honest money, you can ask them if they would at least be willing to let you post a customer input flyer in their merchant storefront window.
 
This page will let you generate a local, neighborhood-focused flyer that you can print and have a merchant post in their storefront window. An in-person customer who wishes to see prices in the merchant's store listed in honest money (ie. Physical Gold, physical Silver, Pirate Chain privacy cryptocurrency), rather than in the local fiat currency (ie. US Dollar), can then scan the QR code on the flyer to cast their vote to provide feedback to prospective merchants, encouraging them to get onboarded into the ZTH Alliance.
 
Once you have generated and printed a single flyer, simply visit a local copy shop and have a bunch of these printed out and ready for each local merchant you visit. Just keep in mind that each flyer is tied not to a specific merchant's shop, but rather the postal code of that neighborhood. Make sure to generate a new, unique flyer for a different postal code in your area. Don't forget to bring some tape, so you can stick the flyer in their storefront window.
